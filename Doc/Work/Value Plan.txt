Description of values
---------------------

name		characteristics connecting creatures valuing it

others		altruists
self		egoists

life		people who prefer to solve conflicts in peaceful and life-preserving ways
death		will resort to violence when facing opposite opinions

order		beings that believe in the power of ordered societies and laws
chaos		individualists, anarchists and generally people who don't care about rules

nature		those who see nature as value in itself
man		those who see nature only as a resource to be exploided by man

the two adjacent to Him/Her. The exact opposite god(dess) is His/Her archenemy,
